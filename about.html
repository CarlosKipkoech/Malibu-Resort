About Us





Malibu Resort, Malindi is a Resort on Malindi Bay at the mouth of the Sabaki River, lying on the Indian Ocean coast of Kenya. It is 120 kilometres northeast of Mombasa.

It boasts lush gardens with a state-of-the-art conferencing facility, luxurious cottages, dining options, swimming, fitness, spa, boating, bird watching, and an unmatched concierge service. This makes us a unique destination, no matter how you define your great escape.

At the shores of the Indian Ocean is an oasis of luxury. Malibu Resort’s new 150 room hotel features state-of-the-art amenities in world-class surroundings. From outdoor recreation to lake-view cocktails; swimming to sunbathing, casual dining to waterfront entertainment, it is the perfect place to rest, play, and make new memories of a lifetime.
